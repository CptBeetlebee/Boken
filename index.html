``` ``` \page # The History of Boken-ka Boken-ka is a tidal-locked moon with a history spanning several thousands of years. Much of it is shrouded in mystery with legends, lies, and myths that are mixed in with actual historical records. The following timeline dates events which have been known to happen. It is sorted as close to chronological order as possible. ## Timeline of Boken-ka ### First Empire (A long time ago) **The First Empire**, A powerful and technologically advanced civilization, possessing automated factories, satalites, engineering, giant robots, and the ability to travel through space. It is unkown of the origin of this empire or how long they existed. It is not clear if they were from Boken-ka or not; regardless, they were advanced to godlike levels of science, magic, and power. **The War of Behemoths**; At some point a war broke out between the First Empire and an unknown enemy. The war was very destructive and used weapons so powerful that their owners began to fear the consequences of using them, such as the Behemoths, giant warforged that tower over the normal races. **The Act of Obedience**; Due to their fear of the Behemoth-sized warforged, the First Empire decided to dispose of them. Taking advantage of their unquestioning loyalty, the First Empire ordered the Behemoths down into a massive pit, and then betrayed their servants by encasing the pit in solid metal. (Possibly included the ordering of suicide among normal-sized warforged, as there are many cases of warforged bodies found within the pits.) **The Shattering**; A time of many calamities, from dangerous celestial activity, to natural disasters, to plagues, to a warforged rebellion (possibly in vengeance for The Act of Obedience). It's unclear exactly what happened, but it brought the great empire to its knees, and humanity to the brink of extinction. The most monumental calamity was "The Shattering" causing the moon to be fractured. The moon was expected to be completely destroyed, however, it is speculated that the destruction was thwarted by a wizard-king who brought together the most powerful spellcasters at the time. The wizard-king, arch-druid, witch-queen, and sorcerer-king of the time used their magic to keep the moon's surface partially intact. The world's surface remain fractured, gravity was altered, and floating land masses became the norm. After everything stabilzed, the world started anew.
### Second Empire (1000+ Years Ago) **The Second Empire is Founded**; Many hundreds or thousands of years after The Shattering, in an effort to redeem themselves in the eyes of humanity for their actions of the past (though the humans had long since forgotten), Beetle and his Warforged followers founded an empire and attempted to rebuild civilization and study the remains of the Ancient Empire. These warforged paved the way for a new wave of technology. The most important invention was the airship. Airships became the staple method of transportation in the new world. **The Second Empire starts to Crumble**; While initially founded with good intentions, in order to keep the peace the empire became more and more dictatorial. Child prisons, crackdowns on piracy and religious cults, and the killing of many of their own innocent citizens in the process greatly inflamed tensions amongst the populace. When a great famine hit, the Second Empire neared its breaking point. **The Fall of the Second Empire**; It is unclear what actually caused the fall of the second empire. The strongest evidence would suggest betrayals from the inside, possibly combined with the rebellion of Dhondallâ€™s followers. But the empire collapsed. The prominent warforged retreated into the skies on their legendary airships, where they reside to this day, forever avoiding the past. At least not in a form recognizable as a Thri-kreen. Whether they mutated from humans, or simply came from a land across the seas, they appeared and have been a resident of our lands ever since. Eventually however, he was slain in battle against an enemy force that legends say outnumbered him hundredfold. After his death he attained near godlike levels of worship from his followers, who want nothing more than to die in a great battle like him. However, their average life expectancy is just 30 years. ***Alignment.*** Thri-kreen value freedom above power, and prefer tightly-knit groups to grand societies. They tend toward Chaotic Neutral. ***Size.*** All thri-kreen share the same light, slender build, though their expected height varies between 4 to 7 feet tall depending on the tribe. Your size is Medium. ***Speed.*** Your base walking speed is 35 feet. ***Extra Arms.*** You have four arms, which can carry items normally and allow you to interact with one extra object for free on your turn (*Player's Handbook*, pg. 190). If you make an attack with a two-handed or loading weapon, you cannot benefit from a shield until the start of your next turn, even if another feature allows you to ignore these properties. ***Sleepless.*** You don't need to sleep, and can stay awake constantly without penalty. You receive the benefits of a long rest even if you spend eight hours engaged in light activity. ***Standing Leap.*** You can always jump as though you made a running start of at least 10 feet. When you take the Dash action, you double the distance you can jump that turn. Each foot you clear on a jump still costs you a foot of movement. ***Languages.*** You can speak, read, and write Common and Thri-kreen. The thri-kreen language is incomprehensible to outsiders, a mixture of mandible clicks and antennae twitches that only a dedicated linguist could mimic. ***Subrace.*** Thri-kreen have spread across the deserts of the world, developing into a diverse array of mantisfolk. Choose one of these subraces. #### Bandit Kreen The so-called mantis bandits stand under 5 feet tall, with a thick set of upper arms. Proud, belligerent, and very visually-focused, they link packs to form warbands which can prey upon outsiders or extort tolls from those crossing the desert, their allegiances marked by shifting carapace-banners. ___ ***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Strength score increases by 1. ___ ***Chameleonic Carapace.*** You can change your body's color to match your surroundings, allowing you to hide when lightly obscured by terrain. In addition, you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) rolls if you have not moved since the start of your last turn.
